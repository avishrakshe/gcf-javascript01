THE SILENT SPIRAL

Personal Mental-State Awareness Companion
Hackathon 2025  |  Category: Mental Health Tech  |  Track: Social Impact
Executive Summary

The Silent Spiral is an AI-powered self-awareness companion designed to help individuals recognize subtle emotional and behavioral patterns before they escalate into mental health crises. By combining guided journaling, NLP-based emotion detection, and personalized pattern recognition, the platform provides a private, non-judgmental space for reflection and early awareness.

The Problem

Mental health challenges rarely appear overnight. They emerge gradually through small, often imperceptible shifts in mood, behavior, and routine. By the time most people recognize a problem, it has already compounded into something overwhelming.

The Awareness Gap

Our Solution
1. Guided Journaling Tools

Contextual daily prompts that gently guide users to articulate feelings they might not have words for. Prompts adapt based on previous entries and detected emotional tone.

"You've mentioned feeling 'tired' a lot this week — is it physical, emotional, or something else?"
"What's one small thing that felt manageable today, even if everything else felt heavy?"

How the Silent Spiral Works

From a journal entry to a meaningful insight — the full technical and experiential pipeline.

01
📓 User Writes a Journal Entry
The user is given a contextual prompt — adapted to their recent emotional tone and time of day. They write freely in natural language. No forms, no checkboxes. The prompt engine selects from 500+ therapeutic-adjacent prompts using a RAG pipeline.
Prompt Engine (RAG) Adaptive prompts
02
🔤 NLP Preprocessing
The text is tokenized, normalized, and cleaned on-device. Colloquial language, regional expressions, and abbreviations are handled. Smart quotes and cultural idioms are preserved to avoid stripping meaning from non-Western expression.
BERT Tokenizer On-device (ONNX)
03
🧠 Emotion Classification
A fine-tuned RoBERTa model (trained on GoEmotions — 27 emotion categories) classifies the entry. Output includes a primary emotion, secondary emotion, and confidence score. The model also flags absolutist language ("always", "never"), social withdrawal phrases, and cognitive distortion patterns.
RoBERTa (fine-tuned) GoEmotions dataset 27 emotion categories
04
📈 Longitudinal Drift Detection
An LSTM-based time series model tracks sentiment polarity across 7, 14, and 30-day windows. Consistent downward drift — even mild — is the hallmark of the "silent spiral." Anomaly detection compares against the user's own personal baseline, not population norms, making detection culturally neutral.
LSTM time-series Personal baseline Not population norms
05
💡 Vocabulary Expansion
When the user uses vague words ("sad", "bad", "fine"), the vocab engine surfaces semantically related, more precise emotion words from the Emotion Wheel taxonomy. This helps users build richer emotional language over time — one of the core drivers of self-awareness.
Emotion Wheel taxonomy Semantic similarity
06
🔔 Gentle Reflection Nudges
When patterns suggest emotional drift (3+ consecutive low-mood entries, increased isolation language, entry frequency drop), the app offers non-alarming nudges: a breathing exercise, a grounding prompt, or a gentle suggestion to connect with someone trusted. Never a clinical recommendation.
Habit-loop model Non-clinical language
07
🔒 Privacy Preserved Throughout
Every step runs entirely on-device. No journal text, emotion label, or behavioral signal ever leaves the phone. AES-256 encrypts the local database. Federated learning updates the model using gradient-only contributions — raw data stays with the user forever.
AES-256 encryption Zero server transmission Federated learning
Key Technical Components

Four pillars powering the awareness engine.

COMPONENT 01
📝
Adaptive Prompt Engine
RAG pipeline over a 500+ prompt library. Selects prompts based on current emotion classification, recent entry patterns, and time-of-day. LLM-assisted variation prevents prompt fatigue. Culturally localized across 5 regional contexts.
COMPONENT 02
🔬
NLP Emotion Pipeline
Fine-tuned RoBERTa for 27-category emotion classification. Runs via ONNX Runtime on-device. Sentiment drift tracked with LSTM across sliding time windows. Linguistic marker rules detect cognitive distortions and withdrawal signals.
COMPONENT 03
📊
Pattern Recognition
Personal baseline modeling with unsupervised clustering. Anomaly detection flags deviations from your own history — not population averages. Entry frequency itself is a signal: reduced journaling correlates with avoidance patterns in depressive states.
COMPONENT 04
💬
Vocabulary Expansion
Emotion Wheel taxonomy with semantic similarity scoring. Maps user's vague terms to precise emotion vocabulary. Tracks vocabulary richness over time as a proxy metric for growing self-awareness — more words = more self-understanding.
